Custom APIs

Gravatar

Enter your email address used in Gravatar.

For example: /gravatar/someone@example.com

Math

/math/[add, subtract, multiply, divide]/value1/value2/...

Examples:

Addition: /math/add/value1/value2/...

Subtract: /math/subtract/value1/value2

Multiply: /math/multiply/value1/value2/...

Division: /math/divide/value1/value2

Sentence Analyzer

Enter a sentence separated by space.

Examples: /analyze/This is a sentence.

Age Calculator

Enter your birthdate(mm/dd/yyyy) to calculate your age.

Examples: /age/04/01/1988
